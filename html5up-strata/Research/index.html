DANIELLA MORRONE

Daniella is a 5th year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, specializing in Astronomy & Physics, minoring in Mathematics and French Language. Having participated in several research programs and currently working to complete a second undergraduate thesis, Daniella is passionate about astronomy, with skills in Python coding language and significant research experience in a diverse range of fields both within and outside Astronomy. Daniella has also been a Teaching Assistant in AST101H1: The Sun and Its Neighbours and AST201H1: Stars and Galaxies, two large-enrolment Astronomy courses at the University of Toronto. Further, Daniella has presented at several research conferences and participated in numerous volunteer and outreach experiences, including leadership programs through the University of St. Michael's College and beyond.

Research Interests: Exoplanets, Astrobiology, Galaxies.
